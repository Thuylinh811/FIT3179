Tuition Costs

Along with fulfilling all application requirements, the cost of attending the school of choice is often the most pressing issue for overseas students.

This chart is an overview of tuiton fees for international students, at the 10 top universities in the Times Higher Education World University Ranking (2015). According to Times Ranking, tuition cost at Harvard Universities ranked at top 1 is approximately at $41,632 per year, which is lower than 10th ranked Columbia University by $9,033 per year.

Overall, tution cost at top universities in The United States is higher than universities in The United Kingdom.

The Shanghai Ranking measures universities scores in different categories

1. Quality of education

  • Alumni: Based on the number of alumni of an institution winning nobel prizes and fields medals.

2. Quality of education

  • HiCi: Based on number of Highly Cited Researchers selected by Thomson Reuters.
  • Award: Based on the number of staff of an institution winning Nobel Prizes in Physics, Chemistry, Medicine, and fields medals.

3. Research output

  • PSC: Based on the number of papers published in Nature and Science.
  • PUB: Based on total number of papers indexed in the Science Citation Index-Expanded and Social Science Citation.

4. Per capita

  • PCP: The weighted scores of the above five indicators divided by the number of full-time academic staff.
